What we do

Terasek One Sdn Bhd (“T-One”), is licensed by the Securities Commission to operate our T-One digital platform for individual and small and medium sized enterprise (“SME”) clients who fall under Part I of Schedules 6 & 7 of CMSA (“Sophisticated Investors”) in Malaysia to invest in:

Initial Public Offering of shares (“IPO Shares”) listed on Bursa Malaysia; and Private Placement of securities by companies (“Placement Securities”) listed on Bursa Malaysia.

Why we're different

T-One’s proprietary platform has been built to improve our bidding efficiency and effectiveness by reaching out to individual and small and medium sized enterprise investors across Malaysia digitally at scale.

Our bidding, payment and allocation processes are fully automated for your convenience.

Custody of Investor's Cash

At T-One, all of our Clients’ funds are held designated bank accounts by [Pacific Trustees Berhad] an independent third party licensed custodian company to safeguard clients’ interest at all times. Insert logo of Pacific Trustees Berhad here.

Clients’ listed securities are directly credited into Clients’ own designated CDS account by the Issuer. T-One do not retain any of Client’s securities.
